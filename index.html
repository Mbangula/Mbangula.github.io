Student Research Symposium (SRS)

The National student research symposium was initiated by a postgraduate student in Computer Science, Lameck Amugongo. The main aims of the SRS are: to give students an opportunity to present and demonstrate their scientific work; equip students with technical and scientific writing skills; enhance focus on applied research; and develop a culture of publishing amongst young researchers.
